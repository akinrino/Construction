Our Projects

From iconic building to educational facilities and centers for learning, from live saving healthcare structures to community infrastructures that brings people together, T-Best Engineering Company is building an enduring future and creating lasting value for its clients. For over half a century, T-Best Engineering Company has delivered projects for government, multinationals, industrial groups, oil and gas majors and high net-worth individuals. Our services include pre-construction, design-build, general contracting and construction management.

We have successfully executed projects of all sizes and complexes such as office buildings; industrial building; commercial building, educational and sports facilities, residential building, public facilities, dredging, shoreline protection, land reclamation and roads and bridges across all geographical area of Nigeria. Our wealth of experience in these areas has earned us recommendations from our clients. With our corporate office in lagos and area offices across the country, the Company is well placed to execute construction projests throughout the country and beyond.
